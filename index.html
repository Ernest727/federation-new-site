Federation vs Current EAC

Current EAC (Today)

  • • Customs union and common market (partial)
  • • Separate national currencies, armies, and laws
  • • Barriers to free movement still exist
  • • Coordination through EAC organs

Proposed Federation

  • • Single sovereign state with federal institutions
  • • Unified market, mobility, and external policy
  • • Common currency & defense structures (phased)
  • • Youth-driven integration and innovation
